What Each Service Does

Dethatching

Power rakes remove dead grass buildup that suffocates your lawn. Lets oxygen reach roots. Grass grows 3x thicker.

Core Aeration

Pulls small plugs from soil to reduce compaction. Water and nutrients reach deep roots. The #1 service for healthy grass.

Fertilizer

Slow-release nitrogen for explosive spring growth. Green-up in 7-10 days. Lasts 8-10 weeks with one application.

Why Bundle?

Aeration opens soil. Fertilizer gets deep into roots. Dethatching removes competition. Together = golf-course results.
