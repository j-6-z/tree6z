Saskatoon Lawn Care by Season

Spring (April–May)

Start with dethatching and aeration. Overseed bare spots. First mow when grass hits 3 inches.

Summer (June–Aug)

Mow weekly, never removing more than 1/3 of blade height. Water deeply 1-2x per week.

Fall (Sept–Oct)

Continue mowing until first freeze. Fall fertilization is crucial. Final mow at 2.5 inches.

Winter Prep

Clear debris to prevent mold. Avoid walking on frozen grass. Plan spring services early.
