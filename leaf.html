Why Fall Cleanup Matters

Prevents Lawn Damage

Leaves left over winter suffocate grass, create mold, and kill root systems. Spring recovery costs way more than fall cleanup.

Protects Gutters

Clogged gutters overflow, freeze, and crack. Water damage to your foundation starts at the roofline.

Eliminates Pests

Leaf piles attract mice, insects, and disease. Clean yards mean fewer unwanted visitors all winter.

Easier Snow Removal

Snow plows and blowers work better on clean surfaces. Buried leaves create slippery, uneven terrain.
